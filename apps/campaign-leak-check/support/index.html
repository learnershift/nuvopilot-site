Campaign Leak Check

Support

Campaign Leak Check is a local-first tool for reviewing possible ad spend leaks from campaign numbers entered by the user.

Contact

Email: support@nuvopilot.com

Support is provided for Campaign Leak Check app usage, local data/export questions, privacy questions, and store listing clarification.

Before Contacting Support

  • Confirm the app is the official Campaign Leak Check listing from NuvoPilot.
  • Do not send legal IDs, bank details, ad account credentials, 2FA codes, customer lists, payment card data, or private campaign account access.
  • If you need to remove local records, open Settings in the app and use the clear-data action.

What To Include

  • App version
  • Device and Android version
  • What screen had the issue
  • Whether the issue happened after input, result, save, export, or settings
  • Screenshots only if they do not contain private or sensitive information

Limits

The submitted v1 target does not connect to advertising platforms and does not manage live campaigns. It provides local estimates and checklists from user-entered numbers.
