Campaign Leak Check

Campaign Leak Check is a local-first Android utility for reviewing possible ad spend leaks from campaign numbers entered by the user.

Current v1 Target

  • No account required
  • No ad account login required
  • No server upload of campaign records
  • No analytics SDK, crash reporting SDK, AdMob SDK, or Billing SDK in the current v1 target
  • User-entered records stay on the device unless the user chooses to export them

Important Limits

Campaign Leak Check provides estimates and checklists based on user-entered numbers. It does not guarantee campaign performance, revenue recovery, platform approval, or financial results.
