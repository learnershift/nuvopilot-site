NuvoPilot

Official website for NuvoPilot mobile app support, privacy information, and developer contact details. NuvoPilot develops local-first Android tools that help marketers and app operators review campaign inputs, store decisions on-device, and keep support controls clear.

Campaign Leak Check

Campaign Leak Check is a local-first utility for reviewing possible ad spend leaks from numbers entered by the user. It does not require an ad account login in the current v1 target.
